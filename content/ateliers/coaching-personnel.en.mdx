  The exercises gently strengthen the abdominal core and improve joint flexibility while correcting posture. Based on a series of dynamic exercises, the work seeks to link the use of body weight with breath. More elaborate choreographic sequences are introduced to help reclaim one's space, reinforce support, and improve coordination, precision and concentration — essential elements for navigating everyday life, especially in a professional context.


  It is not uncommon to gain 1 or 2 cm in height through spinal work, develop a more graceful walk, and relieve lower-back tension (particularly for those who spend long hours seated) — a true relief !


  Starting from the principle that body and mind are intimately connected, the goal is to release tension, expand the range of movement, and affirm one's choices by putting them into motion. It is a form of dialogue — with others and with ourselves.
