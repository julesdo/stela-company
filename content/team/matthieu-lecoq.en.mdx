  - >-
    **Matthieu Lecoq** — Pianist, Cellist & Composer. 2nd prize at the Concours
    Général de musique. Training: degrees in piano and cello at the Pôle
    supérieur Paris Boulogne-Billancourt, musicology at the Sorbonne, and
    master's in improvisation in Jean-François Zygel's class at CNSMDP.
    Currently pianist at the Paris Opera Dance School and CNSMDP.
  - >-
    **Explorations** : Passionate about world music and improvisation, he
    regularly collaborates with traditional musicians (Cameroon, Iran, China,
    Mongolia...). Practices Mongolian throat singing and horsehead fiddle (2nd
    prize at the international competition in Ingolstadt). Member of the Chinese
    Orchestra of Paris, he also explores Chinese dance, Peking opera and Kuaiban.
  - >-
    **Creations** : More than 150 performances with the Mixity company, combining
    music, theatre, poetry, circus and cabaret. Accompanies silent films at the
    Cinémathèque Française. Musical director of the Vent d'orient Vent d'Occident
    association, he creates and composes for the Vent des Cordes quartet. More
    than a hundred concerts per year in prestigious venues (Opéra Garnier, Opéra
    Bastille, Théâtre des Champs-Élysées, Philharmonie de Paris...).
  - >-
    **In Médée ∞ MEDEA** : Live musician and final composer of the first
    creation. After initial research conducted with other musicians, he
    composes the final score and improvises on stage on piano and cello. His
    music creates a living dialogue between tradition and modernity, linking
    text, body and space in a moving sound atmosphere.
