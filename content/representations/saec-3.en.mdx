    ### Presentation

    At the end of November and in exactly 2 months now, I announce the
    programming of this third edition in partnership and collaboration with
    Paris Anim' Maurice Ravel.

    An evening that highlights dance companies from the French choreographic
    scene with a desire for sensitive writing initiated by Cie Mānuka by Thomas
    Brenaa.

    Between tribute, support, companies in distribution, young dancers for a
    simple and rich dance moment that allows you to immerse yourself in worlds.

    ### Participating Companies

    **Cie Mānuka** (Thomas Brenaa) - Sensitive writing

    **Cie L'Inconnu** (Romain Difazio) - "Fractale"

    **La Stela Company** - "Médee ~ Medea"

    **Odile Cougoule** (Lisa Cyv) - "An infinite solitude", "The bird" and "An
    uncertain desire"

    **Cie HORS pISTE** (Mélanie DLZE) - "ASAP (it's quickly said, it's true)"

    **Cie Aran** - "Amy"

    **Loann Pezet** - Heartfelt invitation

    ### Booking
